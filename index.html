Quick Start Guide

Buy BABYRABBIT TOKEN

01
Create MetaMask wallet

Create a MetaMask Wallet using either a desktop computer or an iOS/Android mobile device. That will allow you to buy, sell, send, and receive BABYRABBIT Coin.

02
Send BNB to your wallet

You can buy Binance Coin (BNB) directly on MetaMask or transfer it to your MetaMask Wallet from exchanges like Binance, etc. Make sure to use the BEP-20 network when transfering BNB.

03
Connect your wallet to PancakeSwap

Access your wallet to PancakeSwap by clicking ‘Connect to a wallet’ and selecting MetaMask.

04
Swap BNB for BABYRABBIT TOKEN

You can start swapping as soon as you have BNB available! Press ‘Select a token’ and enter the token address or search for it on the tokens list.
